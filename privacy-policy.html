Course Finder: Golf in Ontario

Privacy Policy — Your privacy matters. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

Effective Date: April 1, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 11, 2026

1. Overview

Course Finder: Golf in Ontario ("the App") is operated by Bryan Laks ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

We are committed to keeping your data minimal. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

📍 Location Data

The App requests access to your device's location for the following purposes:

  • Showing golf courses near you on the map
  • Calculating distances between your current location and courses
  • Powering the distance filter feature
  • Reverse geocoding to display your approximate city or region
Location is only accessed while the App is in use. We do not track your location in the background and do not store your location on our servers.

✍️ User-Submitted Reviews

If you choose to submit a course review, we collect:

  • The name you provide (does not need to be your real name)
  • Your star rating and written comment
  • An anonymous device identifier (see below)
  • The date and time of submission

📱 Anonymous Device Identifier

When you submit a review, the App generates a random identifier and stores it on your device using local storage. This identifier is not linked to your Apple ID, name, email address, or any personal account. Its sole purpose is to allow you to identify and delete your own reviews within the App. It is stored alongside your review in our database.

📧 Support Correspondence

If you contact us via email for support, we receive your email address and any information you include in your message. This is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

📊 Usage Data

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or behavioural data beyond what Apple's standard App Store and TestFlight tools provide automatically.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use information only to:

  • Display nearby golf courses based on your location
  • Provide distance-based sorting and filtering
  • Display community reviews and allow you to manage your own submissions
  • Power the AI Golf Trip Planner feature (see Section 4)
  • Respond to support requests you initiate

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond core app functionality.

4. Data Storage & Third Parties

Supabase

Golf course data and user-submitted reviews are stored in Supabase, a cloud database provider. Reviews include the name you provide, your star rating, your written comment, and the anonymous device identifier described above. This identifier is not linked to your Apple ID or any personal account.

Supabase stores data on servers located in the United States. You can review Supabase's privacy practices at supabase.com/privacy.

Anthropic — AI-Powered Feature

Golf Trip Planner: The App includes an AI-powered Golf Trip Planner feature. When you use this feature, the text you enter is sent to Anthropic, PBC for processing by their Claude AI model. We do not store your AI conversation history on our servers. Anthropic's privacy policy governs how this data is handled: anthropic.com/privacy.

We do not share, sell, or rent any personal information to any other third parties. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs or tracking libraries.

5. Data Retention

Location data is processed on-device and is never transmitted to or stored by us.

User-submitted reviews are retained until you delete them within the App, or until you contact us requesting removal. The anonymous device identifier associated with a review is deleted along with the review.

If you email us for support, your email may be retained for up to 12 months for the purpose of follow-up support, after which it is deleted.

6. Children's Privacy

Course Finder: Golf in Ontario is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights (Canadian Users)

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for collection where applicable
  • Request deletion of your review data
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Security

We use industry-standard HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit. The anonymous device identifier stored with reviews is randomly generated and contains no personal information. We do not store passwords, payment information, or government identifiers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
